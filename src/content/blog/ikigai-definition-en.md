Ikigai, an ancient Japanese concept, originated on the island of Okinawa, famous for having the highest number of centenarians in the world. But Ikigai is not just about physical longevity: above all, it is the secret to a fulfilling and meaningful life.

## The intersection of 4 pillars

Your Ikigai is found at the perfect intersection of four fundamental dimensions:

1. **What you love** (your passion)
2. **What the world needs** (your mission)
3. **What you can be paid for** (your profession)
4. **What you are good at** (your vocation)

When these four circles meet, you find your Ikigai. No more compromising between doing what you must to survive and doing what makes you feel alive.

## Why is it important today?

In a very fast-paced world, many feel disconnected, lost in a comfortable but meaningless "autopilot". Finding our Ikigai is no longer just personal development; it has become an absolute necessity to avoid burnout.
